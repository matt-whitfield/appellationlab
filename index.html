Analytics & Management for Wine Regions

Engagement Analytics

Track how consumers, trade, and media engage with your regional content. Monitor completion rates, popular topics, and learning journeys to measure your platform's impact.

User & Access Management

Control platform access for different audiences. Manage trade credentials, consumer registrations, and media accounts to ensure the right content reaches the right people.

Certification Programs

Issue regional certifications (e.g., "Certified Regional Wine Explorer") to create brand ambassadors and measure educational impact through certification completion rates.

Content Performance

Identify which regional stories, AVA maps, and varietal profiles resonate most with your audiences. Use data to refine your regional narrative and maximize engagement.

Audience Insights

Understand who's engaging with your region—consumers, trade, or media. Track user growth and regional reach to measure brand awareness expansion.

Ambassador Programs

Track regional certification completions and identify brand ambassadors. Measure how certification programs build loyalty and deepen connection to your region.

Flexible Management

Seamlessly update regional content, launch new educational modules, and manage platform access—all through an intuitive admin interface designed for wine region teams.
